Interstellar

2014 169 min 8.6/10

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

Adventure Drama Sci-Fi
Synopsis

A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival as Earth's resources are depleting.

Trivia & Facts
  • The visual effects team consulted with theoretical physicist Kip Thorne to create realistic depictions of black holes.
  • Christopher Nolan planted 500 acres of corn for the film because he didn't want to use CGI.
  • The film won the Academy Award for Best Visual Effects.
