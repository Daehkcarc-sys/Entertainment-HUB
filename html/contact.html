Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the "Sign In / Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar and follow the instructions to create your new account. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a secure password.

How can I add titles to my watchlist?

To add a title to your watchlist, navigate to the movie, series, anime, or game page you're interested in and click the "Add to Watchlist" button. You must be logged in to use this feature. You can view and manage your watchlist from your profile page or by clicking on "Watchlist" in the navigation menu.

How do I submit a review or rating?

To submit a review or rating, navigate to the specific content page and click the "Rate" button. You'll be taken to our ratings page where you can select a star rating and optionally write a review. You must be logged in to submit ratings and reviews.

I found incorrect information on your site. How can I report it?

We strive for accuracy in all our content. If you find any incorrect information, please use the contact form on this page to submit a correction. Include the page URL, the incorrect information, and the correct information if possible.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, log in and go to your profile settings. At the bottom of the page, you'll find an option to delete your account. Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data, including reviews, comments, and watchlists.
