Popular Support Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. The link will expire after 24 hours for security reasons.

How do I manage my subscription?

To manage your subscription, sign in to your account and go to your Profile page. Click on "Subscription" in the settings menu. From there, you can view your current plan, billing information, payment history, and make changes to your subscription.

Why can't I add certain content to my watchlist?

There could be several reasons why you can't add content to your watchlist:

  • You need to be signed in to use the watchlist feature
  • The content may already be in your watchlist
  • You may have reached the maximum limit for your watchlist (Premium accounts have unlimited watchlists)
  • There might be a temporary technical issue

If you continue to experience problems, please contact our support team for assistance.
