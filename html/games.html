Game Details

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki and made in collaboration with fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin, who provided material for the setting.

Key Features:

  • Vast open-world design with six main areas
  • Intricate combat system with diverse weapon styles
  • Rich lore and environmental storytelling
  • Character customization with multiple builds and playstyles
  • Legacy dungeons with complex level design
  • Challenging boss fights that test player skill
